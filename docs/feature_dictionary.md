# Feature Dictionary

## debt_to_income_ratio

**Definition:** Loan amount divided by annual income.

**Business Meaning:** Measures debt burden.

Higher values indicate higher risk.

---

## loan_burden_ratio

**Definition:** Annual loan installment amount divided by annual income.

**Business Meaning:** Measures repayment pressure.

---

## credit_utilization_proxy

**Definition:** Loan amount divided by goods price.

**Business Meaning:** Estimates borrowing dependence.

---

## income_stability_score

**Definition:** Annual income divided by employment years.

**Business Meaning:** Measures income reliability.

---

## income_per_family_member

**Definition:** Annual income divided by family size.

**Business Meaning:** Estimates financial pressure.

---

## credit_inquiry_ratio

**Definition:** Credit inquiries divided by age.

**Business Meaning:** Measures credit-seeking behavior.

---

## ext_score_mean

**Definition:** Average of external credit scores.

**Business Meaning:** Summarizes external risk indicators.
